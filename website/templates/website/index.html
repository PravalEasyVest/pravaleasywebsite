Our Investment Framwork

  • 1. Value

    • • Evaluate the quality of business , earnings and management.

    • To scrutinise the balance sheet, cash flow statements and P&L account.

  • 2. Outlook

    • • Evaluate margin strength, sustainability and nature of growth to determine the most probable growth model.

    • Scrutinise the data enumerating historical growth.

  • 3. Risk/Reward

    • • Model the most probable bottom line improvements to determine upside potential.

    • Eliminate even the best of the businesses if they are not risk reward positive for the client.

  • 4. Human Bias Free

    • • All the above pivots have to be substantiated with reliable data.

    • To automate and not let biases towards or against a particular business hinder returns.

The lessons from over two decades of tutelage under Mr.Markets and the learnings amassed from investment literature form the cornerstone of our investment framework. We believe our framework is like a tree which will evolve with uncertainties of the markets but will always be rooted in Data, Knowledge and Rationality.

Our Algorithm

Asset Allocation

The algorithm evaluates historical and relative valuations of several asset classes and allocates your wealth depending upon the risk appetite and capital adequacy.

Investible Universe

The algorithm then scans through over 85 financial data points of each of the listed stocks to eliminate any business without value or having a negative business outlook.

Customised Universe

The algorithm then scouts the Investible Universe for businesses that are in sync with your risk profile and capital adequacy.

Intelligent diversification

The algorithm then allocates capital among stocks in a way that provides you with the most favourable risk reward ratio

Monitor

The algorithm then tracks your investments 24*7 to notify you of any opportunity or mishap. This helps you in protecting and growing your wealth.
